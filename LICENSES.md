# Licenses and Upstream Attribution

This standalone scaffold is intended to compose two community projects into a Raspberry Pi 5 deployment:

- Frigate Hailo-10H add-on: https://github.com/mikehailodev/frigate-hass-addons-h10
- Hailo VLM add-on: https://github.com/mikehailodev/hailo-vlm-addon
- Frigate: https://github.com/blakeblackshear/frigate

The upstream Hailo-10H add-on repository is MIT licensed. Preserve its license when copying files from it into this project.

HailoRT `.deb` and `.whl` packages are not included here. Obtain them from the appropriate Hailo/Frigate/Hailo community distribution channel and confirm that your use complies with their license terms.
